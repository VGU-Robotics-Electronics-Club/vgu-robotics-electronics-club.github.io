About REC

Founded in 2011, the VGU Robotics and Electronics Club (REC) at the Vietnamese-German University is the leading and only student community in electronics, robotics, and engineering.

With a mission to inspire passion for science and technology, REC serves as a platform for innovation where students explore future technologies, sharpen practical skills, and transform creative ideas into reality.

  • Robot Creation & Design
  • Embedded Systems Programming
  • Organizing Technical Esport Tournaments
