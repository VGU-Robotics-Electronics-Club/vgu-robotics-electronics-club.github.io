VSC - VGU Soccerbot Championship is the annual competition of ECE students as part of the Exploring Engineering course. But now, with the mission of creating opportunities and a playground not only for those passionate about building robots but also for students from other majors who are just starting to get into robotics—or simply want to try out a new, creative, and challenging field.

Inspired by the world’s most popular sport, this competition is the fusion of humans and machines, giving birth to Soccerbot. In every Soccerbot match, each team controls 2 robots: one defender and one attacker. During 6 minutes of intense gameplay, the team that scores the most goals will claim victory!
